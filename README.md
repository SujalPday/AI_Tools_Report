# 📊 AI Tools Report

This report analyses the global demand, adoption, and growth of AI tools from 2022 to 2025. Data 
was collected from three primary sources: the Stack Overflow Developer Survey (developer 
adoption), Google Trends (search demand over time), and Our World in Data (market-level 
indicators including AI job postings, research publications, and company formation). The data was 
processed using Pandas and NumPy, stored in MySQL, and visualised in Tableau.

## 👩🏽‍🍳 The Process

- I collected the data from Google Trends, Our World in Data, and Stack Overflow with the help of Claude AI.
- Processed all the data using Pandas and MySQL.
- Cleaned the data and sorted it on the basis of years.
- Connected the MySQL data with Tableau.
- Did visualisations and created dashboards.
- Analysed the charts and prepared a report using "MS Word".

## 📋 Key Findings

- **AI tool dominates :** ChatGPT dominates the developer AI tools by 85.31%.
- **Fastest growing :** Cursor is the fastest growing AI tool with search interest over 158.82%.
- **Rise in AI jobs :** AI jobs share rose globally from 0.29% to 2.09% from 2014 to 2025. (7x growth)
- **New AI companies :** 6252 new AI companies were founded by 2025.
- **Leading country - Publications :** China leads in AI research publications with 561891 publication by 2025.
- **Growth of AI :** Global AI research grew 14.9% from 2017 ti 2024.
- **Leading country - Jobs :** Singapore leads in AI jobs share at 4.69%.

## ⚙️ Running the Project

To run the project in your local environment:

1. Download these files into your system:
   - [Google Trends](https://github.com/SujalPday/AI_Tools_Report/tree/ea8bec7fbf8fb4216878002a0d627fe1de87d286/Google%20Trends "Google Trends Files"),
   - [OWID](https://github.com/SujalPday/AI_Tools_Report/tree/ea8bec7fbf8fb4216878002a0d627fe1de87d286/OWID "OWID FIles")
   - [Stack Overflow](https://github.com/SujalPday/AI_Tools_Report/tree/ea8bec7fbf8fb4216878002a0d627fe1de87d286/Stack%20Overflow "Stack Overflow files")
   - [Tally](https://github.com/SujalPday/AI_Tools_Report/blob/ea8bec7fbf8fb4216878002a0d627fe1de87d286/AI%20Tools%20Report.twb "AI_Tools_Report.twb")
2. Save all these files in a directory and make sure to sort the sub-directories for ease of operations.
3. Download the "Pandas", "Numpy", "OS" modules for python.
4. Copy the location of the directory where you saved the database file.
5. Open the "pipeline.py" in Google Trends, OWID, and Stack Overflow folders and replace the "[Paste Directory Here]" with your current pipeline folder.
6. Connect the data with Tableau.
7. Perform analysis using visualisations and dashboards.
